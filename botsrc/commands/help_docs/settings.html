Chat Settings Command This command will allow chat administrators to alter the spam protection settings for the given chat Usage Example: /settings [Option] [Value] /settings detect_spam [On/Off/Enable/Disable] /settings detect_spam_action [Nothing/Delete/Kick/Ban] /settings blacklists [On/Off/Enable/Disable] /settings general_alerts [On/Off/Enable/Disable] /settings potential_spammer_protection [On/Off/Enable/Disable] /settings delete_old_messages [On/Off/Enable/Disable] Enable Spam Protection You can enable spam protection in your group by sending the following commands /settings detect_spam on /settings detect_spam_action delete This will detect spam in your chat and remove it accordingly without affecting the user How do i protect my group from unwanted users? You can protect your group from blacklisted users and potential spammers by sending the following commands /settings potential_spammer_protection on /settings blacklists on What are potential spammers? These are users that the bot believes are spammers due to their recent activities in other groups What are blacklisted users? These are users blacklisted by volunteers from the Telegram community because of abusive behavior How do i disable alerts? /general_alerts on How can i let blacklisted users join? /setting blacklists off How can i let potential spammers join? /settings potential_spammer_protection off
